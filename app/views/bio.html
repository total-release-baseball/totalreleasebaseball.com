About Coach Frase

Jeff Frase was a standout athlete at Olathe North High School. He went to Butler County Community College from high school where he started for 2 years on the infield and in the outfield. From BCCC, Jeff went to Fort Hays State University where he started in the outfield for 2 years. Upon completion of college, Jeff immediately started coaching at the high school level where he coached for the following 13 years. He as coached high school baseball at Olathe North, Shawnee Mission West, Blue Valley and Blue Valley Southwest (he even coached football at Shawnee Mission West). Coach Frase has also coached at every level of youth baseball from T-ball on up.

Jeff studied with the Frozen Ropes Hitting Academy personnel and was a previous owner/instructor at Next level Baseball for 10 years. He as organized coaching clinics for the Olathe Youth Baseball program and started player instruction for Olathe Youth Baseball at the age of 18.

Good fundamentals, building confidence and developing a personal relationship with students are all important aspects of the teaching process and vital to skill development. The skills that Jeff will be analyzing, teaching and improving include hitting, throwing, defense and base running.
