Services & Pricing

Total Release Baseball offers several services.

Clinics

Group clinics are held every Sunday from 1pm to 2pm starting October and going through March with the focus alternating weekly between fielding and hitting.

  • $20 - full-hour clinic

Individual Instruction

One-on-one instructional sessions are available during evenings and weekends.

  • $45 - half-hour session
  • $70 - full-hour session
  • $350 - package of 10 half-hour sessions

Team Instruction

Team instructional sessions consisting of practice organization, practice management, practice instruction and coaching suggestions are available during evenings and weekends.

  • $60 - full-hour session

Facility Rental

Indoor and outdoor facilities can be rented for team practices. Check the schedule for availability.
