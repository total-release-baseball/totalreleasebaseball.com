About Coach Frase

Owner/Instructor at Next Level Baseball - 10 years

Studied hitting techniques with many different college coaches at clinics and with instructors at the Frozen Ropes hitting academy (one of the largest in the country).

Coaching Experience

Coach Frase has over 20 years total coaching experience. He started working camps for Olathe Youth Baseball at age 18 and has held positions at the high school level with Olathe North, Shawnee Mission West, Blue Valley and Blue Valley Southwest. He also coached USA tryouts and USA team in St. Louis.

As a Player

Jeff was a standout 3-year letter winner at Olathe North High School. He started for two years on the infield and outfield for Butler County Community College. He went on to start in the outfield for two more years at Fort Hays State University.
